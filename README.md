## Introduction

Kaleidoscope: Tavern is a mod developed for Minecraft Java Edition. This mod is committed to bringing a rich brewing experience and tavern-themed decorative content to your world. Grow grapes, craft fine drinks through a multi-stage fermentation system, and build the perfect tavern atmosphere — all in one mod.

## Features

*   🍷 Complete multi-stage brewing system with 7 quality levels
*   🍾 10+ types of drinks including Wine, Champagne, Whiskey, Vodka and more
*   🌿 Grape farming with wild grapevines, trellises, and a pressing tub
*   🪑 16-color sofas and bar stools (sit-able)
*   🏠 Rich tavern decorations: bar counter, bar cabinet, string lights, paintings and more
*   📋 Writable chalkboard and sandwich board with text alignment options
*   🔥 Throwable Molotov Cocktail

### Brewing System

Grow grapes on trellises or find wild grapevines in the world. Crush your harvest in the Pressing Tub, then fill a Barrel with juice and ingredients to start fermentation.

Your brew's quality evolves over time through 7 levels:

1 Undrinkable  
2 Inferior  
3 Common 4 Fine  
5 Crafted  
6 Exquisite  
7 Vintage

Once ready, attach a Tap to your barrel and extract the finished drink into a bottle.

### Available Drinks

*   Wine, Champagne, Vodka, Brandy, Whiskey
*   Carignan, Sakura Wine, Plum Wine, Ice Wine
*   Vinegar
*   Grape Juice, Sweet Berries Juice, Glow Berries Juice
*   Molotov Cocktail (throwable)

*   Bar Counter — for the classic bar look
*   Bar Cabinet / Glass Bar Cabinet — display your finest bottles
*   Chalkboard & Sandwich Board — write custom text with alignment options
*   String Lights — 17 color variants for ambient lighting
*   Paintings — featuring famous artworks and original art
*   Stepladder — a handy decorative utility block

### Mod Compatibility

*   JEI / REI / EMI — Full recipe category support
*   Jade — Barrel status HUD (brew level, ingredients, next level)
